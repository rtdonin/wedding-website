July 30, 2024

First Conversation

We met via a matchmaker on SawYouAtSinai.com. On our first phone call, Rita thought Matthew was a little pretentious…until she realized he was a nerd just like her. We laughed, talked some more, and texted afterward, both secretly excited about where this might go.

October 29, 2024

First Date

Rita came down to D.C., and we spent our first date walking along the National Mall after dinner. Her feet got blisters, but she barely noticed. We were too busy talking, laughing, and swapping stories. Matthew’s never ending riffing suggesting that the hollow-sounding central grate at the D.C. War Memorial was the entrance to the Mole People Embassy apparently convinced Rita he was the real deal. By the end of the night, we knew we wanted more adventures together.

November 16, 2024

Philadelphia Day Trip

Our third date was a day trip to Philadelphia. We wandered around the city, shared snacks, and laughed at little things only we seemed to notice. We sent each other a postcard from the Museum of the American Revolution. By the end of the day, we were already collecting inside jokes that would stick with us forever.

December 8, 2024

Morgan Library Powered by Starbucks

We visited, for the first time, two locations that we love: the Starbucks Reserve and Roastery (naturally, for anyone who knows Matthew), and The Morgan Library, for lovers of old books and intricate architecture.

December 20, 2024

First Shabbat Together

Rita invited herself over for Shabbat, and in hindsight, we’re both so happy she did. Otherwise, due to various circumstances, it would have been over a month before we saw each other again! Rita met some of Matthew’s friends, and experienced one of his over-the-top feasts, for the first time.

December 27, 2024

Flipper Waddlesworth

For Chanukkah, Matthew sent Rita a stuffed penguin, who is now named Flipper Waddlesworth. Inspired by the song “Personal Penguin” by Sandra Boyton. We decided we want to be each other's personal penguin.

January 10, 2025

Meeting Fanya

Now it was Matthew’s turn to meet Rita’s friends. Rita hosted Shabbat dinner for her friends, and Matthew came up to NYC to attend. That’s the day he met Rita’s sister, Fanya. She approved, of course!

January 11, 2025

Opera Night – La Bohème

The day after Shabbat, we went to the Opera to see La Bohème. Rita's mother claims this was 'torturing' Matthew.

January 12, 2025

Meeting Rita's Parents

Matthew met Rita’s parents for the first time. Stories were shared, jokes landed, and by the end of lunch, it felt like a warm welcome we’d both hoped for. Rita’s mom joked that he doesn’t play an instrument or chess, incorrectly inferring the lack of a tiger mom—but he passed with flying colors anyway. Then we took a stroll to Ouri's kosher grocery store on 68th and 3rd. We both love grocery stores, but Matthew suspected Rita was showing him how great the kosher grocery stores are in New York City as proof of the city’s superiority. “This must be how Yeltsin felt,” he thought to himself.

February 7, 2025

Meeting Matthew’s Parents

Rita met Matthew’s parents for the first time, and later went together to the Kennedy Center to see the National Symphony.

February 14, 2025

Attending a Wedding Together

We attended a wedding together in Manchester-by-the-Sea, Massachusetts on Valentine’s Day. Rita met Matthew’s coworkers and their wives. They approved, and by approved, we mean “Insisted that Matthew lock that down immediately.”

March 4, 2025

Brother’s Wedding

Matthew met Rita’s whole family and community all in one go at her brother Ben’s wedding. He survived the gauntlet of new people, the blizzard of names, and the language barriers. Rita's brother's Father-in-law even jokingly said we should have our wedding the day after.

May 22, 2025

The Proposal

After a delay acquiring a ring involving the disruption of international trade, Matthew proposed to Rita at Next Door, an Israeli restaurant near the Grove in LA. She said yes, of course!

June 29, 2025

L’chaim!

We celebrated with a Brooklyn-based Engagement Party. Surrounded by friends, family, and happiness, it was a perfect day to toast the start of our next chapter together.
